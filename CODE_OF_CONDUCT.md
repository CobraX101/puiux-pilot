# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to welcome@puiux.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.
